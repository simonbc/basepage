---
title: Digital gardens
---

A digital garden is a personal wiki you tend over time — notes that grow and link to
each other, rather than dated blog posts you publish once and forget.

The mechanics are simple: write notes, connect them with [[Wikilinks]], and let the
backlinks reveal the structure. Start small with a note like [[Welcome]] and grow
from there.

Your garden, your files. Keep it private as local memory, or publish it.
