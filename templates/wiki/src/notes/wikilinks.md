---
title: Wikilinks
---

A wikilink is a page reference wrapped in double square brackets, like
`[[Welcome]]`. Basepage resolves it to the note whose title matches.

Change the visible text with a vertical bar: `[[Welcome|the first note]]` renders as
[[Welcome|the first note]] but still points at the same page.

Wikilinks are what turn a folder of notes into a [[Digital gardens|digital garden]] —
a connected web instead of a flat list.
